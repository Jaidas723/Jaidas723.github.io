About Rao Brothers

STEEL IS OUR
LANGUAGE

Rao Brothers was built on the shop floor, not in an office. With over 15 years of hands-on fabrication based in Usman Koria, we've welded everything from intricate residential gates to load-bearing solar mounting systems. We don't outsource. We don't cut corners. Every piece that leaves our workshop carries our name — and we take that seriously.

Our team combines old-school trade knowledge with modern finishing and coating technology. Whether you need a simple window grill or a full architectural steel staircase, we approach it the same way: measure twice, weld once.

Structural integrity certified on all load-bearing work
Custom design from your sketch or specification
Powder coat, galvanizing, and raw-steel finishes
On-site measurement and installation available
